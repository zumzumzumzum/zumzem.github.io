What are the most important warnings in life?


  1. On Money
    1. If you start spending more than you earn, cut down spendingimmediately.
    2. If you can’t seem to attract money, work on yourself rather than money itself.
    3. If you buy things just to impress people, you could be anyone’s slave.
  2. On Social
    1. If people hate you, maybe you’re doing the wrong thing.
    2. If people hate you, maybe you’re doing the right thing.
    3. My point is, do what you think is right -- maturely and responsibly.
  3. On Mind
    1. If you live in your mind all the time, get out and retouch your physical reality. You’re a human being.
    2. If you worry all the time, you need to worry about that. Well…as you can obviously see, worry is an never-ending cycle which doesn’t work. Eliminate worry.
    3. If you feel hopeless, don’t be hopeless. In the long run, things get better.
  4. On Attitude
    1. If you blame others, you may subtly justify your wrongdoings.
    2. If you expect luck to fall on you, good luck waiting. Create your own luck is a wiser move.
    3. If you belittle little things, don’t be surprised why you can’t achieve big things.
  5. On Fulfillment
    1. If you dread Mondays, you dread work. Life’s wasted.
    2. If you dread waking up, you dread life. Life’s wasted.
    3. If you feel time passes slowly, it’s because you’re waiting for it to pass. Do something fulfilling. Now.

Pay attention to warnings and make necessary adjustments.

Warnings are, in essence, our silent mentors.
